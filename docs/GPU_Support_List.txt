【🛡️ Why Your GPU Will Likely Work Too?】

This bundle is not specifically modified for particular GPUs, but rather 
based on KataGo's official OpenCL backend with compatibility hardening, 
featuring the following "anti-crash" characteristics:

  · Automatically cleans BOM encoding anomalies produced by Windows Notepad (prevents crashes)
  · Intelligently detects VRAM size and automatically caps maxBatchSize (prevents VRAM overflow)
  · Automatically identifies AMD/NVIDIA/Intel driver versions and downgrades to conservative parameters (prevents timeouts)

Even if your GPU model is not listed above, the script will automatically 
switch to "conservative fallback mode" during startup, ensuring the software 
opens normally without direct crashes.
